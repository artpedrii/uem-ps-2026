# Paddle Duel

Paddle Duel is a local two-player arcade game inspired by classic table tennis games. Each player controls one paddle and tries to prevent the ball from passing their side of the screen.

The left paddle is controlled by Player 2, and the right paddle is controlled by Player 1. The ball starts in the center of the screen and moves across the arena. When the ball passes one side, the opposite player scores a point.

## Controls

Left player controls the left paddle using E/D.

Right player controls the right paddle using the arrow keys.

Only the up and down directions are used during gameplay.

## Gameplay

Move your paddle up and down to block the ball. When the ball hits a paddle, it bounces back toward the other player. When it hits the top or bottom of the screen, it bounces vertically.

The ball speed starts slowly and can increase during the match, making the game harder over time.
